## Next Steps

To refine the improvement plan, additional production data is required, including:
- Resin type and supplier specifications
- Scrap classification and frequency
- Machine parameters (temperature, pressure, cycle time)
- Environmental conditions (humidity, storage)

A structured process audit checklist will be developed to identify the root cause before capital investment.
