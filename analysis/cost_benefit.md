# Cost–Benefit Analysis – Aerospace Injection Molding Improvements

This document provides a structured cost–benefit evaluation of the proposed improvement strategies for aerospace composite injection molding. The analysis is generated using Google Gemini and is aligned with AS9100 and FAA/EASA quality and safety requirements.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

Each improvement is evaluated across four dimensions:

- **Quality** — Impact on defect reduction and part consistency  
- **Cost** — Investment required (equipment, training, tooling)  
- **Safety** — Influence on compliance and risk mitigation  
- **Efficiency** — Effect on cycle time, throughput, and labor  

---

## Cost–Benefit Table

| Improvement | Quality | Cost | Safety | Efficiency | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| In-Mold Sensors | High | Medium | High | Medium | Enables real-time cavity monitoring and traceability |
| Desiccant Monitoring | High | Low | High | Low | Prevents moisture-related defects in hygroscopic resins |
| Conformal Cooling | Medium | Medium | Medium | High | Improves thermal uniformity and reduces warpage |
| AI Vision System | High | High | Low | High | Automates defect detection and reduces human error |
| MFI Lot Testing | Low | High | Medium | Low | Prevents bad resin batches from entering production |
| Scientific Molding | High | High | High | Medium | Establishes a robust process window |
| Ultrasonic NDT | Medium | Low | High | Low | Ensures weld-line integrity for safety-critical parts |
| Cobot Handling | Medium | Medium | Low | High | Standardizes cooling time and reduces handling damage |

---

## Summary

High-impact, low-cost improvements:

- **Desiccant Monitoring**
- **In-Mold Sensors**

High-impact but higher investment:

- **AI Vision System**
- **Scientific Molding**

Low-impact but essential for compliance:

- **Ultrasonic NDT**
- **MFI Lot Testing**

---

## Recommendation

A phased implementation is recommended:

1. **Phase 1 (Low cost, high impact):**  
   - Desiccant monitoring  
   - In-mold sensors  

2. **Phase 2 (Medium investment):**  
   - Conformal cooling  
   - Cobot handling  

3. **Phase 3 (High investment, long-term ROI):**  
   - AI vision system  
   - Scientific molding  

This structured approach balances cost with measurable improvements in quality and safety.
